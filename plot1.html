Plot 1: Max Temperature

Plot 1: Max Temperature

Plot Description

This plot shows the correlation between a city's distance from the equator and its maximum recorded temperature.

In general, there is a negative correlation between distance from the equator and maximum recorded temperature - so as expected, cities closer to the equator reach higher temperatures.
